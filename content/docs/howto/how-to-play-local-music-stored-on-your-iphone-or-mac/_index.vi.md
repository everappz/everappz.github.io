**Tom tat:** Cai dat [Evermusic](/products/evermusic) (MP3/WAV) hoac [Flacbox](/products/flacbox) (FLAC/DSD), mo bat ky tap tin am thanh hoac thu muc cuc bo nao va bat dau phat. Them thu muc vao **Yeu thich** de truy cap nhanh, nhap cac ban nhac vao Thu vien Nhac cua ban, hoac tao **Danh sach phat**.

Trong thoi dai ky thuat so, cac dich vu phat truc tuyen da thay doi cach chung ta nghe nhac, nhung co dieu gi do dac biet ve nhac ma chung ta da suu tam qua nhieu nam tren Mac, PC hoac o dia ngoai.

Nhung giai dieu cuc bo quy gia nay mang y nghia ca nhan va su hoai niem. May man thay, viec phat nhac nay tren iPhone va Mac cua ban van co the va thu vi. Du ban muon song lai ky niem hay thuong thuc bo suu tap doc dao cua minh ngoai tuyen, huong dan nay se chi cho ban cach thuc hien.

Chung ta se kham pha cac phuong phap va cong cu de phat nhac cuc bo cua ban mot cach muot ma tren cac thiet bi Apple, bien chung thanh phong hoa nhac ca nhan cho nhung giai dieu yeu thich. Neu ban san sang khai pha tiem nang cua iPhone va Mac cho nhac cuc bo, hay bat dau hanh trinh am nhac nay cung nhau!

## Buoc 1: Bat dau voi Evermusic (cho dinh dang am thanh tieu chuan) hoac Flacbox (cho nhac khong mat du lieu)

De bat dau hanh trinh vao the gioi nhac cuc bo tren iPhone va Mac, hay bat dau bang cach cai dat Evermusic (cho cac dinh dang am thanh tieu chuan nhu mp3 va wav) hoac Flacbox (cho nhac khong mat du lieu trong dsd va flac). Ca hai ung dung deu co san cho iOS va MacOS, va ban co the tai chung mien phi.

## Buoc 2: Phat tap tin cuc bo tren thiet bi cua ban

Sau khi cai dat ung dung, mo man hinh "Local Files" va cuon xuong phan "Files on this iPhone". Tu do, chon "Open files..." neu ban can chon nhieu tap tin am thanh hoac "Open folder..." neu ban muon chon mot thu muc nhac. Ung dung se quet noi dung thu muc va tat ca cac tap tin am thanh tim thay se duoc chon.

{{< figure src="/docs/howto/how-to-play-local-music-stored-on-your-iphone-or-mac/21260c_ec30fd738d6b46eb9dba21f51c7f1f3e~mv2.png" alt="evermusic local files screen" width="400" >}}

Tren man hinh tiep theo, nhan vao muc "Browse" tren thanh tab phia duoi va cuon den phan "Locations". Chon muc menu "On My iPhone".

{{< figure src="/docs/howto/how-to-play-local-music-stored-on-your-iphone-or-mac/21260c_67b3f80c255b4be59588bfab8d978588~mv2.png" alt="system files picker" width="400" >}}

Dieu huong den thu muc nhac cua ban va nhan "Open" de xac nhan lua chon thu muc hien tai.

{{< figure src="/docs/howto/how-to-play-local-music-stored-on-your-iphone-or-mac/21260c_40b43e5713954f10a2f8843abed491d7~mv2.png" alt="music folder content" width="400" >}}

Ngoai ra, neu ban da chon "Open files..." ban dau, hay chon cac tap tin ban muon mo va nhan "Open" de xac nhan lua chon cua ban.

{{< figure src="/docs/howto/how-to-play-local-music-stored-on-your-iphone-or-mac/21260c_af4a7b0543444fe6adac9f33403c1153~mv2.png" alt="selected audio files" width="400" >}}

Sau khi nhan "Open," trinh phat am thanh toan man hinh se xuat hien va cac tap tin se duoc them vao hang doi phat. Cac tap tin nay se duoc phat truc tiep tu vi tri da chon ma khong duoc sao chep vao goi ung dung. Neu ban muon sao chep tap tin vao ung dung moi khi mo chung, hay dieu huong den **Cai dat** - "File manager" - "Copy files during opening" va chon "Always copy files during opening."

{{< figure src="/docs/howto/how-to-play-local-music-stored-on-your-iphone-or-mac/21260c_38eec9a335904d1899f47e89a6e78287~mv2.png" alt="audio player" width="400" >}}

## Buoc 3: Them thu muc tren thiet bi cua ban vao Yeu thich de truy cap nhanh

Ban co the don gian hoa qua trinh bang cach them thu muc tren thiet bi cua ban vao **Yeu thich**. Bang cach nay, ban khong can lap lai cac buoc tren moi khi muon phat nhac. De thuc hien dieu nay, mo man hinh "Local Files" va cuon den phan "Quick access". Nhan **Yeu thich** de truy cap man hinh "Favorite files".

{{< figure src="/docs/howto/how-to-play-local-music-stored-on-your-iphone-or-mac/21260c_4ce5bbe2e4f5407f9f30fd520de2c7fa~mv2.png" alt="favorites, recents, quick access section" width="400" >}}

Nhan nut **Them hanh dong** (duoc bieu thi bang ba dau cham) o goc tren ben phai va chon muc menu "Add folder".

{{< figure src="/docs/howto/how-to-play-local-music-stored-on-your-iphone-or-mac/21260c_f662e350a6ac4b568913e0a8d2ab92e9~mv2.png" alt="add favorite folder" width="400" >}}

Nhan "Files on this iPhone" de mo trinh chon tap tin he thong.

{{< figure src="/docs/howto/how-to-play-local-music-stored-on-your-iphone-or-mac/21260c_5e2625989c6d4bf7ad45e747b42ef6ba~mv2.png" alt="select folder for favorites" width="400" >}}

Dieu huong vao ben trong thu muc chua cac tap tin am thanh cua ban va nhan "Open" de xac nhan lua chon cua ban.

{{< figure src="/docs/howto/how-to-play-local-music-stored-on-your-iphone-or-mac/21260c_10affade5c9b4bdd95ddea26088a38cf~mv2.png" alt="files picker" width="400" >}}

Thu muc cua ban se duoc them vao "Favorite files," giup ban truy cap nhanh vao nhac cua minh.

{{< figure src="/docs/howto/how-to-play-local-music-stored-on-your-iphone-or-mac/21260c_e495cae3bfc7456fb13de1e3d13f0682~mv2.png" alt="folder added to favorites" width="400" >}}

3. Tren man hinh tiep theo, chon muc menu "Local files".

4. Cuon xuong phan "Files on this iPhone" va nhan "Open files..."

## Buoc 5: Them cac tap tin cuc bo tu iPhone vao danh sach phat

De them cac tap tin cuc bo tu thiet bi cua ban vao **Danh sach phat**, hay lam theo cac buoc sau:

1. Mo man hinh **Danh sach phat**.
2. Nhan nut them o goc tren ben phai.
3. Chon "+ New playlist" tu menu.

   {{< figure src="/docs/howto/how-to-play-local-music-stored-on-your-iphone-or-mac/21260c_c7d893af6424434aa4f5ffff81170c07~mv2.png" alt="more actions for playlist" width="400" >}}

4. Nhap ten cho **Danh sach phat** moi cua ban.

   {{< figure src="/docs/howto/how-to-play-local-music-stored-on-your-iphone-or-mac/21260c_282c085da817422f90638b4312849658~mv2.png" alt="create new playlist - enter a name" width="400" >}}

5. Tren man hinh tiep theo, chon tuy chon "Local files".

   {{< figure src="/docs/howto/how-to-play-local-music-stored-on-your-iphone-or-mac/21260c_d96d75b3ba334eb48665ae170aebffeb~mv2.png" alt="add songs to playlist - select sources" width="400" >}}

6. Cuon xuong phan "Files on this iPhone" va nhan "Open files..."

   {{< figure src="/docs/howto/how-to-play-local-music-stored-on-your-iphone-or-mac/21260c_d20cf14ed00f402b819060a3b99ed61e~mv2.png" alt="local files chooser screen" width="400" >}}

7. Chon cac tap tin am thanh ban muon them va nhan "Open" de xac nhan lua chon cua ban.

   {{< figure src="/docs/howto/how-to-play-local-music-stored-on-your-iphone-or-mac/21260c_40bfea8b45b542d0a3f92f59203d461d~mv2.png" alt="selected audio files" width="400" >}}

8. Cac tap tin se duoc them vao **Danh sach phat**, va ban co the thay doi thu tu bai hat va thuc hien cac hanh dong khac bang nut them o goc tren ben phai.

   {{< figure src="/docs/howto/how-to-play-local-music-stored-on-your-iphone-or-mac/21260c_7a22420b2fcc43a59c745b93e7fb53e6~mv2.png" alt="playlist with local files screen" width="400" >}}

## Ket luan

Voi nhung buoc don gian nay, ban co the khai pha toan bo tiem nang cua iPhone va Mac nhu nen tang tot nhat de thuong thuc bo suu tap nhac cuc bo quy gia cua ban. Hay tam biet nhung han che cua dich vu phat truc tuyen va kham pha lai su ky dieu cua **Danh sach phat** va bai hat do ban tu chon. Hanh trinh am nhac cua ban chi cach vai lan cham!

## FAQ

{{% details title="Evermusic va Flacbox co the phat nhung dinh dang am thanh nao?" closed="true" %}}
Evermusic phat MP3, WAV, AAC, M4A va cac dinh dang tieu chuan khac. Flacbox them ho tro cho FLAC, DSD, OGG, OPUS, APE, WMA va ALAC.
{{% /details %}}

{{% details title="Cac ung dung nay co sao chep tap tin vao bo nho ung dung khong?" closed="true" %}}
Theo mac dinh, tap tin duoc phat tu vi tri goc ma khong bi sao chep. De thay doi hanh vi nay, bat "Always copy files during opening" trong **Cai dat** > File manager.
{{% /details %}}

{{% details title="Toi co the sap xep nhac cuc bo theo nghe si va album khong?" closed="true" %}}
Co. Nhap tap tin vao Thu vien Nhac (Buoc 4) va ung dung doc sieu du lieu de nhom cac ban nhac theo Nghe si, Album, The loai va Nha soan nhac.
{{% /details %}}

{{% details title="Lam the nao de chuyen nhac tu may tinh sang iPhone?" closed="true" %}}
Su dung iTunes File Sharing (USB), WiFi Drive (khong day) hoac SMB (phat truc tuyen). Xem huong dan chi tiet cua chung toi: [Chuyen va phat tap tin cuc bo tren iPhone](/docs/howto/how-to-play-local-itunes-files-on-my-iphone/).
{{% /details %}}

{{% details title="Evermusic va Flacbox co mien phi khong?" closed="true" %}}
Co, ca hai ung dung deu mien phi tai ve voi cac tinh nang cot loi bao gom phat nhac, bo can bang va phat truc tuyen tu dam may. Cac phien ban mien phi co mot so han che (so luong **Danh sach phat**, tai khoan dam may, thu muc ngoai tuyen). Nang cap len Premium se xoa bo cac han che nay.
{{% /details %}}
