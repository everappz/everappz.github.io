## Evermusic: The Cloud Music Player for iPhone and iPad

**TL;DR:** Evermusic lets you stream and download music from 12+ cloud services (Dropbox, Google Drive, OneDrive, and more) directly on your iPhone or iPad. Watch the official promo video below to see the app in action.

Evermusic is a cloud music player that connects to your personal cloud storage and turns it into a full music library. The official promo video, produced by [**Ilya Agulov**](https://instagram.com/agulov_), shows the core experience: streaming from the cloud, downloading for offline playback, and organizing your collection.

## Key Features Shown in the Video

- **Multi-cloud support** -- connect Dropbox, Google Drive, OneDrive, MEGA, and 8+ other services
- **Offline playback** -- download tracks from the cloud and listen without internet
- **Music library manager** -- automatic metadata scanning, album art, and organized browsing
- **Built-in equalizer** -- fine-tune your audio with presets or custom settings
- **CarPlay and AirPlay** -- seamless playback across Apple devices

[Download Evermusic free on the App Store](https://apps.apple.com/app/evermusic-cloud-music-player/id885367198) and try it with your own cloud music library.

## Frequently Asked Questions

{{% details title="What is Evermusic?" closed="true" %}}
Evermusic is a cloud music player for iPhone and iPad. It connects to cloud storage services like Dropbox, Google Drive, and OneDrive, letting you stream and download your own music files. It supports FLAC, MP3, AAC, WAV, and other audio formats.
{{% /details %}}

{{% details title="Is Evermusic free to download?" closed="true" %}}
Yes. Evermusic is free to download with basic features. A one-time Premium upgrade unlocks the equalizer, crossfade, and additional cloud account connections.
{{% /details %}}

{{% details title="What cloud services does Evermusic support?" closed="true" %}}
Evermusic supports over 12 cloud platforms including iCloud Drive, Dropbox, Google Drive, OneDrive, Box, MEGA, Yandex.Disk, pCloud, and any server running WebDAV or SMB protocols.
{{% /details %}}
